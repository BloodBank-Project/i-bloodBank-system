DONOR GUIDELINES
  • Donor should be physically healthy and should be between 18-65 years.
  • Donor should have not donated blood within the previous 3 months.
  • Donors typically need to weight at least 110 pounds (50 kilograms) to donate blood safely.
  • Donors should not have a cold, flu, sore throat, cold sore, stomach bug, or any other infection.
  • Donors should not have recently had a tattoo or body piercing
  • Donors should not have heart disease or ailment.
  • Donors should not have asthma, epilepsy, thalassemia, sickle cell anaemia, polycythemia vera, or similar conditions.
  • Donors should avoid alcohol for 24 hours.
